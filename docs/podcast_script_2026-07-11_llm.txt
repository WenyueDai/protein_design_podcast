It refines what we can measure and how we reason, but it does not yet overturn the foundational principles of protein‑ligand recognition, folding energetics, or evolutionary selection that have guided protein design since the 1970s. The field remains in a phase of paradigm refinement: powerful new instruments are being bolted onto an established theoretical scaffold, accelerating discovery without rewriting the scaffold itself.
