A fun cross‑domain experiment would be to take a published ML model trained on PDB‑derived protein‑ligand poses, fine‑tune it on the host‑guest subset of the CSD, and then ask it to predict pose rankings for a set of ten host‑guest pairs with known ITC affinities. Tracking how the enrichment factor changes with the amount of host‑guest data could reveal whether protein‑centric features transfer cleanly or need re‑weighting.

All of these threads feel like they could be pursued with modest compute — a few GPU hours for the ablation, a weekend’s worth of CSD downloads for the host‑guest test — and they might just surface the next useful tweak for the design pipelines we’re already building.
